What is Knowledge?

Introduction to Epistemology

Learning Objectives

  • Understand what epistemology studies
  • Distinguish between knowledge and belief
  • Recognize why knowledge matters

Key Terms

Epistemology: The study of knowledge
Knowledge: Justified true belief
Belief: What we think is true

Lesson Content

Imagine you're looking outside and see wet streets. You think to yourself, "It must have rained." But how do you know this? What if someone just washed their car? This simple example shows us the difference between believing something and actually knowing it.

Epistemology is the branch of philosophy that asks these big questions about knowledge. Philosophers have been trying to figure out what knowledge really is for over 2,000 years!

Think about these everyday situations:

  • You "know" 2+2=4, but how?
  • You "know" your friend's name, but what if you misremembered?
  • You "know" the Earth is round, but have you seen it yourself?

Knowledge seems different from just believing or guessing. When we say we "know" something, we usually mean we're confident it's true and we have good reasons for thinking so.

想像你正看著窗外，看到濕漉漉的街道。你心想：「一定下雨了。」但你怎麼會知道這件事呢？如果只是有人剛洗完車呢？這個簡單的例子向我們展示了相信某事和真正知道某事之間的區別。

認識論（Epistemology）是哲學的一個分支，它探討關於知識的這些大問題。哲學家們兩千多年來一直在努力弄清楚知識到底是什麼！

思考這些日常情境：

  • 你「知道」2+2=4，但怎麼知道的？
  • 你「知道」你朋友的名字，但萬一你記錯了呢？
  • 你「知道」地球是圓的，但你自己親眼見過嗎？

知識似乎與單純的相信或猜測不同。當我們說我們「知道」某事時，我們通常意味著我們確信它是真的，並且我們有充分的理由這樣認為。

Think About This

Can you think of something you believed was true but later found out was false? What made you change your mind? This shows why philosophers care so much about the difference between knowledge and belief!

你能想到你曾經相信是真的，但後來發現是假的事情嗎？是什麼讓你改變了主意？這說明了為什麼哲學家如此關心知識和信念之間的區別！

Lesson 2: Individuality and Existence

What are Individual Things?

Individual things are entities that exist independently, separate and distinct from other things in the world. However, what counts as "separate" is not always clear.

個體事物是獨立存在的實體，與世界中的其他事物分離和區別。但是，什麼算作「分離」並不總是很清楚。

📝 Lesson 2 Quiz

Multiple Choice: Which theory claims that only one individual thing exists?

Lesson 3: Properties and Relations

What are Properties?

Properties are characteristics that things have. For example, redness, roundness, or being tall. But do properties exist independently of the things that have them?

屬性是事物所具有的特徵。例如，紅色、圓形或高大。但是屬性是否獨立於具有它們的事物而存在？

📝 Lesson 3 Quiz

Short Answer: Give an example of a property and explain whether you think it exists independently.

Lesson 4: The Mind-Body Problem

What is the Mind-Body Problem?

How does the mind relate to the body? Are they the same thing, or are they different? This is one of the most important questions in philosophy.

心靈如何與身體相關？它們是同一個東西，還是不同的？這是哲學中最重要的問題之一。

📝 Lesson 4 Quiz

Multiple Choice: Which theory claims that mind and body are completely separate substances?

Lesson 5: Free Will and Determinism

Do We Have Free Will?

If everything in the universe follows physical laws, do we really have free will? Or are all our choices determined by prior causes?

如果宇宙中的一切都遵循物理定律，我們真的有自由意志嗎？還是我們所有的選擇都由先前的原因決定？

📝 Lesson 5 Quiz

Reflection: Do you think you have free will? Explain your reasoning.

Lesson 6: God and the Universe

Does God Exist?

This lesson explores different arguments for and against the existence of God, including the cosmological argument and the problem of evil.

本課探討支持和反對上帝存在的不同論證，包括宇宙論證和邪惡問題。

📝 Lesson 6 Quiz

Multiple Choice: The cosmological argument is based on which principle?

About This Course

Course Origin

This Introduction to Metaphysics course began in 2024, designed by me, Yu Jiang Eugene Chang, for middle school children's home schooling education. As a Taiwanese professional engineer and Chairman of the AI Forensic Engineering Institute, I deeply realized during the teaching process that with the rapid development of AI technology, many traditionally considered "fundamental" knowledge has become more important than ever.

這個形上學入門課程始於2024年，由我張渝江為國中孩子的自學教育(home schooling)所設計。作為一名台灣的工程師土木技師兼AI工程鑑定學會的理事長，我在教學過程中深刻體會到，隨著AI技術的快速發展，許多傳統上被認為是「基礎」的知識變得比過去更加重要。

Why Metaphysics?

In an era where AI can handle increasingly technical tasks, humanity's most precious abilities are deep thinking, critical thinking, and exploration of fundamental questions. Metaphysics, as a core branch of philosophy, is the best way to cultivate these abilities. It not only trains logical thinking but, more importantly, cultivates curiosity and humility toward the world.

在AI能夠處理越來越多技術性工作的時代，人類最珍貴的能力反而是深度思考、批判性思維，以及對根本問題的探索。形上學作為哲學的核心分支，正是培養這些能力的最佳途徑。它不僅訓練邏輯思維，更重要的是培養對世界的好奇心和謙卑態度。
